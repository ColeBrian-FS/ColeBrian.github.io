The Lab

Summary

The application I decided to create for this month is The Lab: Fighting Game Stat Checker. In this application, The user will have the ability to tracks match history ( Wins & Losses), Combo Damage, Best/Worst Match up for specific characters and watch live tournaments happening across the globe. The Lab is build for the FGC (fighting game community )player that is a new challenger or a current player trying to grinding out in training mode or in their player versus player matches.
